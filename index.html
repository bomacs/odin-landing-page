What is I Photo?

Image by: F1Digitals

I Photo is a company that provides opportunities to freelance photographers who are finding possible clients online. It is like a hub for talented photographers who wants to earn money to add up their income. An easy to go place for someone who is looking for talented photographers to cover their event.

Our vision is to be the leading online platform in photography by providing quality services both to photographers and customers.

Our mission is to promote high class quality of talents in photography, making photographers' world class talents known to the people. At the same aspect to help clients covered their memorable and special events by finding photographers of their choice in our platform.
